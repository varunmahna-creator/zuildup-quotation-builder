===== SLIDE 7:  =====
Waterproofing: Double Protection System
Water damage is preventable with the right approach. Our multi-layer waterproofing system provides redundant protection in all vulnerable areas, backed by rigorous testing before handover.
Basement Protection
Level 1: Crystalline compound mixed into concrete that swells to seal micro-pores when water is encountered
Level 2: RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) walls for beam are robust, structural, or non-structural elements composed of concrete reinforced with steel bars to withstand heavy compressive and tensile loads.
Level 3 : Guniting Waterproofing on RCC Wall using dry mix shotcrete technique & then 4’6” brick work with Seamless cement plaster
Terrace & Mumty Shielding
Level 1: Waterproof concrete slab with waterproofing chemical mixed into the concrete.
Level 2: Surface coating on slab and 2ft up parapet walls
Testing: 24-hour standing water flood test on entire terrace
Bathroom & Balcony
Level 1: Concrete toe walls form watertight bowl with sealed joints
Level 2: Waterproof coating on surfaces.
Testing: 24-hour water retention test in each space
We use premium brands including Sika, Penetron, Kryton, and Pidilite, with application following manufacturer specifications. Additional protection includes waterproofing chemicals in external plaster and plumbing sleeves cast directly into concrete for watertight pipe bonds.
