# Basement/Foundation Protection  [style=Heading 4]
Level 1: Crystalline compound mixed into concrete that swells to seal micro-pores when water is encountered
Level 2: RCC walls — robust structural elements of concrete reinforced with steel bars to withstand heavy compressive and tensile loads
Level 3: Guniting Waterproofing on RCC Wall using dry mix shotcrete technique & then 4'6" brick work with seamless cement plaster

# Terrace & Mumty Shielding  [style=Heading 4]
Level 1: Waterproof concrete slab with waterproofing chemical mixed into the concrete
Level 2: Surface coating on slab and 2ft up parapet walls
Testing: 24-hour standing water flood test on entire terrace

# Bathroom & Balcony  [style=Heading 4]
Level 1: Concrete toe walls form watertight bowl with sealed joints
Level 2: Waterproof coating on surfaces
Testing: 24-hour water retention test in each space
