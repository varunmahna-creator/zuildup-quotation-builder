Per-Line-Item Rate Overrides (optional)

Override the rate for a specific item (e.g. Terrace at ₹650 even though Zone C default is ₹600). Leave blank to use the zone default above.

When on, balcony bills as N rows (one per floor) at independent rates. Blank cells fall back to the Zone B default. PDF collapses to one row if all rates match.

GST and any liaisoning can be added below as a separate zone, or quoted outside this document.

Area Overrides (optional)

Override the computed area of any line item (e.g. 2nd floor at 50% coverage). Leave blank to use the auto-calculated area.

Additional Charges (optional)

Append flat lump-sum charges to the cost sheet. Each enabled charge becomes the next sequential zone letter (e.g. Zone F) and is included in the construction total.
